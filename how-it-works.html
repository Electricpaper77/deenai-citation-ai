What gets blocked

  • Fake citations

  • High-risk fatwa or ruling requests
  • Citation bypass requests
  • Sectarian bait

Render rule

DeenAI can show a normal answer placeholder only when source IDs are valid, citation requirements are satisfied, and claim mappings pass. Otherwise the UI must show a refusal, escalation, source-missing, or verifier-failed state.
